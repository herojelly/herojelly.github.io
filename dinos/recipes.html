    Classic Nuggets
    Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
    Lay out frozen nuggets on a sheet pan.
    Place the sheet pan in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
    Orange Nuggets
    Combine soy sauce, orange juice, brown sugar, and orange zest to make a soy sauce mixture.
    Prepare the nuggets using the Classic Nuggets recipe above.
    In a hot pan with oil and garlic, add ginger, red pepper flakes, soy sauce mixture, and rice wine vinegar.
    Add the nuggets to the pan and coat with sauce.
    Teriyaki Nuggets
    Combine soy sauce, water, vinegar, garlic, honey, and red pepper flakes to make a soy sauce mixture.
    Prepare the nuggets using the Classic Nuggets recipe above.
    In a hot pan, add the soy sauce mixture.
    Add the nuggets to the pan and coat with sauce.
